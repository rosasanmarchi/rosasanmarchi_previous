Rosa SanMarchi is a Chicago based actor. She's a member of SAG-AFTRA and is represented by Grossman & Jack Talent.

Rosa began her acting career in her hometown of Phoenix, AZ. She spent several years in Los Angeles where she studied with Cameron Thor and Alice Carter at the Carter Thor Studio. During her time in LA she acted in, directed, wrote and produced numerous on-camera and theatrical projects.

Rosa is now proud to be a Chicago actor working in film, TV and theater.

Currently she is performing in "4Play sex in a series" at the Den Theatre.
